Loot can be collected all over the surface world. Various items are needed for tasks such as Crafting, Quests and Workshop Upgrades. Some common ones can be picked up anywhere on the map, but particular zones are rich in particular kinds of loot.

Loot Categories by Zone

Industrial Zones

Best for: Batteries, Gears, Electronic Components, and Toolsets. Look for factories and warehouses. Essential for crafting ammunition and explosives.

Medical Areas

Best for: Antiseptics, Syringes, Bandages, and Stim-shots. Check field hospitals and pharmacies. PvP hotspots as every squad needs meds to survive.

Residential Sectors

Best for: Dog Collars, Valuables, Food, and Cosmetic items. Explore houses and apartment blocks. Great for solo players looking for quick credit items.

Security Outposts

Best for: Weapon Parts, Ammo, Access Keys, and Armor Plates. Expect heavy resistance. Keys or hacking tools are often required to access main loot rooms.
